Official Info & Validity

What exactly is NIOS?

The National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) is an autonomous board operating under the Ministry of Education (MHRD), Government of India.

It provides a legal, flexible, and fully recognized alternative for students to complete their schooling. It holds the exact same validity as CBSE, ICSE, or GSEB.
